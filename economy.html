Economy & Industry

Korat is a major economic hub of Isan, driven by agriculture, logistics, manufacturing, and tourism.

  • Agriculture: cassava, sugarcane, rice, and livestock.
  • Industry: food processing, construction materials, and automotive parts.
  • Services: retail, education, and medical facilities serving the wider region.
