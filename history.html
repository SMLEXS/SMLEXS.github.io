Origins & Timeline

Nakhon Ratchasima has served as a strategic frontier city for centuries, linking the Chao Phraya basin with the Isan plateau.

  • Ancient settlements flourished around Phimai with Khmer influence.
  • Ayutthaya & Rattanakosin: Korat became a key military and administrative hub.
  • Modern era: A thriving province driven by agriculture, industry, and tourism.
