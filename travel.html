Travel & Accommodation

Korat is about 260 km from Bangkok with multiple transport options.

  • By car: ~3–4 hours via Motorway 6 / Mittraphap Road (traffic-dependent).
  • By bus: Frequent services from Bangkok’s terminals.
  • By train: Scenic routes to Nakhon Ratchasima Station.

Accommodation ranges from boutique hotels in the city center to eco-resorts near Khao Yai.
